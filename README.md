## Who I am

I am a software engineer that got my started in mathematics and spent an early portion of my career doing Houdini and Cinema4D development. Later in life I began working in the Shopify ecommerce industry and led the software develoments for a new Canadian cosmetics manufacturer startup that within a few years became the leading Shopify supplier for beauty brands on the Shopify App Store. Today I build for order editing and we process over 10 billion dollars of orders each year and over a million monthly edits on orders, a leader in the industry. With experience in Clickhouse, AWS, and GraphQL I now lead a team of engineers building the frontier of scaling order routing operations for a majority of D2C brands.
